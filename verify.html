Verify Certificate

This page allows employers, clients, and institutions to verify the authenticity of certificates issued by the Indian Photographic Society. Each certificate carries a unique verification code.

Enter the certificate code exactly as printed on the certificate to confirm its validity, issuance date, and certification details.
