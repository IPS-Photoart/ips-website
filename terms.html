Terms and Conditions

1. General

The Indian Photographic Society (IPS) operates an online photography certification platform. By registering for, appearing in, or downloading any certificate, the candidate agrees to comply with these Terms and Conditions.

2. Examination & Certification

  • All examinations are conducted online under controlled digital environments.
  • Certificates are issued only upon successful completion of the prescribed assessment.
  • Certificates are digitally verifiable, non-transferable, and issued in electronic format.

3. Fees & Payments

  • All examination and certificate fees are non-refundable unless otherwise stated.
  • Certificate download is enabled only after successful payment confirmation.

4. Examination Misconduct Policy

Any act that compromises the integrity of the examination process shall be treated as misconduct.

  • Impersonation or identity misrepresentation
  • Use of unfair means, unauthorized assistance, or prohibited AI tools
  • Copying, sharing, or attempting to leak examination content

5. Consequences of Misconduct

  • Immediate cancellation of examination attempt
  • Withholding, cancellation, or revocation of certificate
  • Permanent disqualification in severe or repeated cases

6. Limitation of Liability

IPS certifications do not guarantee employment, professional assignments, or financial gain. Certificates only attest to examination performance at the time of assessment.

7. Final Authority

Decisions of the Indian Photographic Society regarding examinations, certification, misconduct, and verification shall be final and binding.

8. Privacy Policy

Indian Photographic Society respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address during registration
  • Assessment and certification details
  • Payment-related information handled securely by authorized payment gateways

How We Use Information

Information is used only for providing educational services, issuing certificates, verification, and official communication.

Data Protection

IPS does not sell, trade, or misuse personal data. Payment details are not stored on IPS servers.

9. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Payments made towards certification and examination fees are generally non-refundable.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only in cases of duplicate payment or technical error, subject to internal review and verification.

Certificate Issuance

Once a certificate is issued and made available for download, no refund shall be processed.
