About Our Certification

The Indian Photographic Society is committed to promoting photographic excellence through structured assessment and credible certification. Our programs are designed to evaluate fundamental and professional competencies in photography using a transparent, merit-based examination system.

Certificate Offered in Professional Photography Fundamentals

This three-level certification system is designed for ameatures and semi-professional photographers seeking structured validation of skills and a credible certificate for clients and institutions.

Level 1 – Basic Photography

Duration: 30 minutes

25 MCQs

Exposure triangle, ISO, aperture, shutter speed

Fee: ₹199

Level 2 – Intermediate Composition & Lighting

20 MCQs + 5 short answers (AI graded)

High/low key, framing, positive & negative space

Fee: ₹199

Level 3 – Professional Photography Fundamentals

Completion Certificate

Advanced analytical MCQs + short answers

Fee: ₹199 or ₹500 (₹500 for all 3 Levels certificates together:- Level- 1,2 &3)

Our Motto

The Indian Photographic Society recognises photography not merely as a technical skill, but as an expressive art and a social responsibility. We are committed to maintaining integrity in certification, protecting the credibility of photographers, and promoting ethical artistic practices that contribute meaningfully to society, culture, and visual documentation of our times.
